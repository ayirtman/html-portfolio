The Best Movies According to Batuhan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Fight Club

An absolute classic! I love the mind-bending plot and the gritty, underground vibe.

Son of Rambow

A wonderfully quirky and heartwarming story about friendship and imagination. This movie hits all the right nostalgic notes.

The Schindler's List

A powerful and emotional film that reminds us of the strength of the human spirit even in the darkest times.
